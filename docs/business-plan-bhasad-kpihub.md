# Bhasad and TheKPIHub Business Plan

## Objective

Build a single-operator media and analytics business that uses Bhasad for audience growth and TheKPIHub for productized analytics, dashboards, consulting, and automation services.

## Audience

- Founders and operators who need clearer metrics.
- Creators and small teams adopting AI workflows.
- Business owners who want practical dashboards without enterprise complexity.
- Professionals who need plain-English explanations of automation, analytics, and growth.

## Offers

| Layer | Offer | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | YouTube explainers, templates, checklists | Audience and trust |
| Entry | Dashboard templates and audit kits | Low-friction revenue |
| Core | TheKPIHub analytics setup | Productized implementation |
| Premium | Automation and KPI operating system | High-ticket transformation |
| Retainer | Monthly KPI review and optimization | Recurring revenue |

## Distribution

- YouTube long-form videos for authority.
- Shorts for discovery and repeated exposure.
- bhasad.org for owned traffic and conversion.
- GitHub issues for repeatable production workflows.
- TheKPIHub case studies for proof.

## Funnel

1. Viewer discovers a Bhasad video.
2. Description points to bhasad.org.
3. Website routes to a checklist, template, or consultation.
4. Qualified users move into TheKPIHub offers.
5. Retainers turn dashboard work into monthly operations.

## KPI Dashboard Needs

- Video views, retention, click-through rate, and subscriber conversion.
- Website visits, source mix, and conversion events.
- Lead source, offer interest, and sales stage.
- Revenue by offer, customer type, and acquisition channel.
- Delivery capacity and recurring workload.

## Content Cadence

- Two long-form videos per week.
- Five Shorts per week.
- One website post or lead magnet update per week.
- One weekly business review using the KPI dashboard.
